30352. Struggle for power

Maratha Empire — Not random

Will happen within 300 days of December 30, 1705
Checked again every 300 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1710)

Description

After the death of Rajaram, his wife Tara Bai was ruling on behalf of her son Shivaji II. The Mughals released Shahu from captivity in 1708 and a struggle for power ensued. As a result the Maratthi's Kingdom split into two: Shivaji and Tara Bai had to flee to Kolhapur, and Shahu remained in Satara.

Actions

A. Rule from Satara

  • Centralization -1
  • Offensive Doctrine +1
  • Stability -1

B. Rule from Kolhapur

  • Centralization -1
  • Offensive Doctrine -1
  • Serfdom +1
  • Stability -2
  • Monarch Tarabai (Shivaji II) will never rule
  • Monarch Shahu I will never rule
  • Monarch Rajaram II will never rule
  • Monarch Shahu II will never rule
  • Monarch Pratap Singh will never rule
  • Monarch Shahu Shivaji I ° becomes active
  • Monarch Shahu Shambhaji II ° becomes active
  • Monarch Jiji Bai Sahib ° becomes active
  • Monarch Shahu Shivaji II ° becomes active
  • Monarch Sambhaji II ° becomes active
