293002. Inheritance of Teke

Teke — Not random

Conditions

  • Ottoman Empire exists
  • The following must not occur:
    • Ottoman Empire is a vassal of Teke
  • Control Teke

Will happen within 10 days of February 2, 1423
Checked again every 10 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after December 30, 1433)

Description

When the Byzantines released Mustafa Celebi as a pretender to the Ottoman throne, Teke and many other Ghazi states supported him against Sultan Murad II. The ruler of Karaman, wishing to take advantage of the strife, pushed Osman Bey to invade Anatolia. While he was gathering his forces, Hamza Bey, appointed by Bayezid, ambushed Osman Bey in 1423 and killed him. The Ottomans occupied the Beylik of Teke.

Actions

A. If only we were stronger, we might have done something...

  • Cede Teke to Ottoman Empire
  • Event 301142 - Second Uprising of Fruzhin for Ottoman Empire is triggered immediately

293000. The Ottoman rule is returned

Teke — Not random

Conditions

  • Ottoman Empire exists
  • None of the following must occur:
    • Ottoman Empire is a vassal of Teke
    • Country has at least 5 non-colonial provinces
  • Own Mentese
  • The following must not occur:
    • Event 301017 - Absorption of Aydin for Ottoman Empire has already occurred

Will happen within 30 days of January 2, 1426
Checked again every 30 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1430)

Description

The emirate of Aydin was first conquered by the Ottomans in 1390. Soon after Aydin was re-instated as an independent country by Timurlane, it found itself under the cloak of the Ottoman state again. One of the ghazi states to have supported a pretender to the Ottoman throne, the coastal state of Aydin was permanently annexed in 1426.

Actions

A. We join the Ottoman state (End Game)

  • Event 301017 - Absorption of Aydin for Ottoman Empire is triggered immediately

B. A distant cousin shall fight for the rulership of our lands

  • Stability -2
  • -100 relations with Ottoman Empire
  • Start a war with Ottoman Empire
  • A random province revolts

293001. The will of Yakub Bey

Germiyan — Not random

Conditions

  • Ottoman Empire exists
  • The following must not occur:
    • Ottoman Empire is a vassal of Teke
  • Control Germyan

Will happen within 10 days of February 2, 1429
Checked again every 10 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after December 30, 1439)

Description

When the Byzantines released Mustafa Celebi as a pretender to the Ottoman throne, Germiyan and many of the other ghazi states supported him against Sultan Murad II. However, when Mustafa was murdered in 1423, in a flash of divine inspiration, Yakub Bey, ruler of Germiyan, realized that he had been in the wrong and decided to develop a good relationship with the Ottomans. Never having any sons, Yakub willed his country to the Ottoman state pursuant to his death in February of the year 1429.

Actions

A. We join the Ottoman state (End Game)

  • Event 301018 - Inheritance of Germiyan for Ottoman Empire is triggered immediately

B. A distant cousin shall fight for the rulership of our lands

  • Stability -2
  • -100 relations with Ottoman Empire
  • Start a war with Ottoman Empire
  • A random province revolts
