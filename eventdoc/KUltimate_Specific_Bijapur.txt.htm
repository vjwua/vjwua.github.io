30343. Sunni or Shi'a

Bijapur — Not random

Conditions

  • Monarch Yusuf Adil Shah is active
  • At least one of the following must occur:
    • State religion is sunni
    • State religion is eastern_sunni

Will happen within 400 days of January 2, 1502
Checked again every 400 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1505)

Description

In 1502 the ruler of Bijapur, feeling the rising power of Shi'a Persia and Shah Ismail, as well as a pressure from some of the nobles at his court, has converted to Shi'a religion.

Actions

A. Convert to Shi'a

  • Change religion to eastern_shia
  • Stability -1
  • Innovativeness +1
  • +100 relations with Safavid Empire
  • -60 relations with Mughal Empire
  • -60 relations with Delhi

B. Remain Sunni

  • Stability +1
  • -60 relations with Safavid Empire
  • Innovativeness -1

30350. Rise of the Mahrattis

Bijapur — Not random

Conditions

  • The following must not occur:
    • Maratha Empire exists

Will happen within 3000 days of December 30, 1677
Checked again every 3000 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after December 30, 1685)

Description

Shivaji was a great general in the service of the last ruler of Bijapur, Sikandar Adil Shah. After the Mughal invasion Bijapur was annexed, but Shivaji escaped and started the fight against the Mughals and Islamic domination in south central India.

Actions

A. Surrender to Mahrattis onslaught

  • Flag graphics set to default
  • Become Maratha Empire
  • Change religion to hindu
  • Centralization -4
  • Land +2
  • Offensive Doctrine +1
  • Aristocracy +2
  • Stability -2
  • Revolt risk value in Ahmadnagar -15
  • Revolt risk value in Konkan -5

360000. The Sultanate of Bijapur

Bijapur — Not random

Triggered by

Action A of 30332 - Disintegration of the Bahmani Sultanate for Bahmanid Empire

Description

After the false accusation and execution of Mahmud Gawan, the Bahmani Sultanate slowly fell into decline. In 1489-90 the governors of Ahmadnagar and Bijapur declared their independence, and Bidar followed up soon after. The ruler of the Bahmani Sultanate became a mere figurehead in the hands of the cunning Qasim Barid. The last sultan disappeared on his way to Mekkah, and with him the Bahmani dynasty came to an end.

Actions

A. Who can stop us?

  • +10000 infantry in the capital province
  • +10000 cavalry in the capital province
  • -200 relations with Vijayanagar
  • -150 relations with Bahmanid Empire
  • Break vassalization with Bahmanid Empire
  • Change religion to eastern_sunni
  • marathi will become an accepted culture
  • Event 410057 - The Sultanate of Bijapur for Bahmanid Empire is triggered immediately
