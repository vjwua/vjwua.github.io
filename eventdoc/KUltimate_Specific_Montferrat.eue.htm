456001. Blessed Marguerita di Savoia

Montferrat — Not random

Will happen within 180 days of November 24, 1464
Checked again every 180 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after December 31, 1470)

Description

Marguerita was born as the eldest of the four daughters born to Amadeus of Savoy, (Signore of Piedmont and titular Prince of Achaea) and his wife Catherine of Geneva. On January 17, 1403, Marguerita married Theodore II, Marquis of Montferrat. This marriage lasted for fifteen years but was childless. Theodore died in 1418. Her piety, already great, increased after she had heard the preaching of Vincent Ferrer, who spent several months in Montferrat. Therefore, when she was left a widow in 1418, she decided to abandon the world. Leaving the direction of the affairs of the marquisate to Gian Giacomo, the son of her husband by his first marriage, she retired to Alba where she joined the Third Order of St. Dominic. A little later, Filippo Maria Visconti, Duke of Milan, asked her hand in marriage and begged Pope Martin V to relieve her of her vow. But Marguerita opposed a formal refusal to this request and thoroughly resolved to give herself entirely to God.

Actions

A. A holy woman!

  • Stability +1
  • +10 victory points

On the contrary we can choice Flaminio, illegitimate son of Marquis Giangiorgio Sebastiano of Montferrat, as our ruler.
