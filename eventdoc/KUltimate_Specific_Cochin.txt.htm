355005. Invasion

Cochin — Not random

Conditions

  • At least one of the following must occur:
    • Event 338445 - Portuguese aid the rebellious Raja of Cochin for Cochin has already occurred
    • Event 338449 - Portuguese aid the rebellious Raja of Cochin for Cochin has already occurred
  • The following must not occur:
    • Event 364001 - The Treachery of Cochin for Calicut has already occurred
  • All of the following must occur:
    • Country is at war
    • The following must not occur:
      • Portugal and Cochin are at war
  • Portugal exists

Will happen within 30 days of March 2, 1503
Checked again every 30 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after December 30, 1523)
unless prevented by
Action A of 355001 - Samoothiri Invasion for Cochin

Description

Having abandoned its proper overlord in favor of Portugal, Cochin is attacked in an attempt to resubjugate the wayward principality.

Actions

A. We were almost free...

  • Stability -2
  • Event 364001 - The Treachery of Cochin for Calicut will never fire
