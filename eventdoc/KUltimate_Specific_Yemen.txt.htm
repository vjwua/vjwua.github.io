321000. The Imamate of Sharaf al-Din

Aden — Not random

Will happen within 20 days of January 2, 1507
Checked again every 20 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 30, 1507)

Description

The Zayidi State in the Yemen had been in state of disorder bordering on anarchy from the mid-thirteenth century until 1507, when Imam Sharaf al-Din was elected. During his long reign, Sharaf al-Din would subdue rebellious elements within the polity while saving his state from repeated invasions from both the north and the south.

Actions

A. I forsee Better Days Ahead

  • Centralization +2
  • Stability +3
  • +6000 infantry in the capital province

321001. The Defeat of Mutahhar

Aden — Not random

Conditions

  • At least one of the following must occur:
    • Ottoman Empire owns Urdun
    • Ottoman Empire owns Qusayr
  • The following must not occur:
    • Ottoman Empire is a vassal of Aden
  • Monarch Mutahhar is active

Will happen within 15 days of November 2, 1572
Checked again every 15 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after November 30, 1572)

Description

The Ottoman Campaign in the Yemen of 1571-72 resulted in the defeat and subsequent death, of illness, in November 1572 of the Imam Mutahhar and subjection of the Zayidi State. The rest of the Saadi line of Imams will be prisoners in Constantinople.

Actions

A. We Cannot Escape the Turkish Umbrella

  • Monarch Local Imams * will never rule
  • Monarch Turkish Governors becomes active
  • Event 301102 - Expedition to the Yemen for Ottoman Empire is triggered immediately

B. We Will Continue the Fight!

  • Lose 5000 troops in a random province
  • Lose 5000 troops in a random province
  • Stability -6
  • -200 relations with Ottoman Empire
