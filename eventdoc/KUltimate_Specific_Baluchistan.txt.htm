123002. Raiders

Baluchistan — Not random

Conditions

  • Country is not at war
  • The following must not occur:
    • Stability is at -1 or higher

Will happen within 30 days of March 2, 1419
Checked again every 30 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after December 31, 1819)

Description

The vast lawless stretches of Central Asia were frequently home to bandits and raiders on the lucrative east-west trade routes.

Actions

A. Support the raiders

  • +2500 cavalry in a random province
  • +100
  • Stability +1
  • -100 relations with Timurid Empire
  • -100 relations with Mughal Empire
  • -100 relations with Safavid Empire
  • -100 relations with Gujarat
  • -100 relations with Delhi
  • -100 relations with Khorasan
  • -100 relations with Fars

B. Crush the raiders

  • Global revolt risk +3 for 60 months
  • A random province revolts
