153000. Duo Plorantes Saecula Croatiae

Croatia — Not random

Conditions

  • Ottoman Empire owns Bosna
  • Event 3353 - The City of Men's Desire for Ottoman Empire has already occurred

Will happen within 120 days of January 2, 1453
Checked again every 120 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1700)

Description

The fall of Byzantium opened the way for Ottoman expansion into Balkans, and when the Ottomans conquered Bosnia Croatia become the first line of defence of Christian Europe. The fall of Bosnia marked the beginning of almost 400 years of constant clashes with the Ottomans, and the first 200 years were later named The 2 Crying Centuries of Croatia, describing all the suffering Croatia endured in that long conflict.

Actions

A. Help us, oh Lord!

  • +150 relations with Papal States
  • +150 relations with Austria
  • +150 relations with Spain
  • -200 relations with Ottoman Empire
  • +10 national manpower
  • Land tech investment: +300
  • Infrastructure tech investment: -200
  • Trade tech investment: -200

153001. Antemurale Christianitatis

Croatia — Not random

Conditions

  • Event 153000 - Duo Plorantes Saecula Croatiae for Croatia has already occurred
  • Ottoman Empire is a neighbor
  • At least one of the following must occur:
    • State religion is catholic
    • State religion is counterreform
  • Relations with Papal States are at -50 or higher

Will happen on January 2, 1519

Description

During Ottoman inroads in Europe, Pope Leo X honoured the Croats by calling them 'the strongest shield and the outer walls of Christianity' (Scutum saldissimum et antemurale Christianitatis). He also sent large sums of money and lot of war material to Croatia.

Actions

A. Our thanks go to Rome

  • +20 national manpower
  • +100
  • +200 relations with Papal States
  • +100 relations with Austria
  • +100 relations with Spain
  • +5000 infantry in the capital province
  • +1000 cavalry in the capital province
  • +10 artillery in the capital province

153002. The Peasants' Uprising of 1573

Croatia — Not random

Will happen within 60 days of January 2, 1572
Checked again every 60 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1574)

Description

During 1572 the peasants, disgruntled by the mistreatment by Hungarian nobles, complained to Ban and Emperor, but to no avail. In protest, the peasants stopped paying their dues, to which the nobles responded by fielding a mercenary army. However, the rebels defeated the mercenaries and Croatian Parliament pronounced them traitors. That sparked a general rebellion with an agenda of overthrowing feudal order, and instating peasant government that will take care of taxes and the defense gainst Ottomans. Even though the rebellion was planned for almost a year, it was crushed with the help of Uskoks in three battles in February 1573, and their sole surviving leader, Ambroz Matija Gubec, cruelly executed as an example.

Actions

A. Crush the rebellion

  • Centralization +1
  • Global revolt risk +6 for 18 months
  • Land tech investment: +500
  • Stability -3

B. Compromise

  • Stability -1
  • Serfdom -1

C. Give into the peasants' demands

  • Centralization -2
  • Serfdom -2
  • Stability +1
  • +7000 infantry in the capital province
  • +3000 cavalry in the capital province
  • -1 base tax value in Hrvatska
  • -1 base tax value in Slavonjia

153003. The Uskok resettlement

Croatia — Not random

Conditions

  • Event 326002 - Uskok Piracy for Venice has already occurred
  • Event 326004 - Protestant Aid for Venice has already occurred
  • The following must not occur:
    • Venice and Croatia are at war
  • At least one of the following must occur:
    • Venice owns Istria
    • Venice owns Dalmazia

Will happen within 1 days of January 2, 1614
Checked again every 1 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1622)

Description

The Uskoks were refugees from the Balkans who settled in the town of Senj on the Dalmatian coast, from where they could protect the frontier. Their piracy led to war between Croatia and Venice. As part of the peace, the Uskoks were forcibly relocated away from the coast.

Actions

A. Resettle the Uskoks

  • +2 base manpower in Hrvatska
  • +50 relations with Venice
  • +30 relations with Ottoman Empire
  • -5 national manpower
  • Lose 3000 troops in a random province
  • Naval tech investment: -500
  • -1 base manpower in Istria
