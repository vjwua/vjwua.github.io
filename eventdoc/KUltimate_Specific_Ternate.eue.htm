308000. Founding of the Sultanate

Ternate — Not random

Conditions

  • None of the following must occur:
    • State religion is sunni
    • State religion is eastern_sunni

Will happen within 2000 days of January 2, 1470
Checked again every 2000 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after December 31, 1819)

Description

With the founding of the Sultanate of Ternate, the area became a Muslim Kingdom.

Actions

A. Found a great Muslim dynasty

  • Change religion to eastern_sunni
  • Religion in the capital province changes to sunni

B. Do not found the sultanate

  • Stability -2

308002. Tidore submits to VOC

Ternate — Not random

Conditions

  • Netherlands exists
  • The following must not occur:
    • Ternate and Netherlands are at war
  • Event 3503 - Vereenigde Oostindische Compagnie for Netherlands has already occurred

Will happen within 30 days of January 2, 1667
Checked again every 30 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1668)

Description

Speelman expedition forced the Sultan of Tidore (now free of Spanish presence) to submit to the VOC. A peace treaty was signed between Ternate and Tidore, now both under VOC control.

Actions

A. Submit to VOC (End Game)

  • Break vassalization with Portugal
  • Break vassalization with Spain
  • Event 186421 - Tidore submits to VOC for Netherlands is triggered immediately

B. Attack VOC!

  • Break vassalization with Portugal
  • Break vassalization with Spain
  • -200 relations with Netherlands
  • Start a war with Netherlands
