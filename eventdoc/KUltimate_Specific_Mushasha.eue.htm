139003. Independence of Khuzistan

Musha'sha' — Not random

Conditions

  • Musha'sha' is a vassal of Timurid Empire

Will happen within 30 days of March 2, 1419
Checked again every 30 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after December 31, 1819)

Description

Fiercely independent, the Musha'sha'ids of Khuzistan quickly abandoned their tributary status to the Timurid Empire.

Actions

A. Great!

  • Stability +1
  • -300 relations with Timurid Empire
  • Break vassalization with Timurid Empire

139002. Musha'sha'ids of Khuzistan

Musha'sha' — Not random

Conditions

  • Event 338492 - Shi'as in Musha'sha' for Khuzestan has already occurred
  • The following must not occur:
    • Event 338493 - Musha'sha'ids of Khuzistan for Khuzestan has already occurred

Will happen within 30 days of March 2, 1419
Checked again every 30 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after December 31, 1819)

Description

The Musha'sha'ids were a Shi'a movement, who proclaimed themselves to be the Manifestation or Precursor to the Expected One.

Actions

A. Great!

  • Stability +1
  • +100
  • Revolt risk value in Khuzestan -10
  • +1 base tax value in Khuzestan
  • +100 relations with Irak
  • +100 relations with Kara Koyunlu
  • +100 relations with Basra
  • persian will become an accepted culture
