422000. The Rising of Bilarab bin Nasir

Ghara — Not random

Conditions

  • Oman is a neighbor
  • Event 248006 - Bilarab al-Yarubi assumes the Imamate for Oman has already occurred

Will happen within 30 days of January 2, 1722
Checked again every 30 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after June 30, 1737)

Description

In Response to the usurpation of Bilarab al-Yarubi in Oman, dissidents at the capital persuaded Bilarab bin Nasir, brother-in-Law to the late Imam Sultan ibn Sayyif, to raise the standard of revolt with the aid of the Banu Ghafir.

Actions

A. Down with the Murderous Fiend

  • Masqat will be considered a national province
  • Sohar will be considered a national province
  • +5000 infantry in a random province
  • +5000 cavalry in a random province
  • -200 relations with Oman

B. Turn Over the Traitor

  • +100 relations with Oman
  • Stability -2
  • Event 254011 - Yarubis Request Intervention for Safavid Empire will never fire

Ghara — Not random

Conditions

  • Event 422002 - Victory of the Ghafir for Ghara has already occurred
  • The following must not occur:

With Oman under their thumb, the Ghafir may now claim Oman as their own.

  • somali will become an accepted culture
  • Benadir will be considered a national province
  • Brava will be considered a national province
  • Malindi will be considered a national province
  • Mombasa will be considered a national province
  • Mombasa will be considered a national province
  • Zanzibar will be considered a national province
  • Kilwa will be considered a national province

B. No thanks

  • Centralization -2

422002. Victory of the Ghafir

Ghara — Not random

Conditions

  • At least one of the following must occur:
    • All of the following must occur:
      • Oman owns Masqat
      • Control Masqat
      • Oman owns Sohar
      • Control Sohar
    • All of the following must occur:
      • Oman owns Masqat
      • Control Masqat
      • The following must not occur:
        • Oman owns Sohar
    • All of the following must occur:
      • The following must not occur:
        • Oman owns Masqat
      • Oman owns Sohar
      • Control Sohar
    • All of the following must occur:
      • Own Masqat
      • Control Masqat
      • The following must not occur:
        • Oman exists
    • All of the following must occur:
      • Oman owns Sohar
      • Control Sohar
      • The following must not occur:
        • Oman exists
    • Oman is a vassal of Ghara

Will happen within 360 days of January 2, 1722
Checked again every 360 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1749)

Description

Having run down the last of the Hinawi faction, the Ghafir and their allies now rule Oman.

Actions

A. Assume the Mantle of Oman

  • Masqat will be considered a national province
  • Sohar will be considered a national province
  • Inherit the realms of Oman
  • Event 254011 - Yarubis Request Intervention for Safavid Empire will never fire
  • Event 248007 - The victory of Sayyid Ahmad for Oman will never fire

B. Continue to Rule as the Ghafir

  • Masqat will be considered a national province
  • Sohar will be considered a national province
  • Inherit the realms of Oman
  • Event 254011 - Yarubis Request Intervention for Safavid Empire will never fire
  • Event 422003 - Claim the Omani Sultanate for Ghara will never fire
  • Event 248007 - The victory of Sayyid Ahmad for Oman will never fire

422001. The Defeat of the Ghafir

Ghara — Not random

Triggered by

Action A of 248027 - The Defeat of the Ghafir for Oman

Description

Having evicted the Persians from Oman, Sayyid Ahmad completed the pacification of Oman by routing the Ghafir at Farq in 1749.

Actions

A. Accept Sayyid as Overlord

  • Masqat will no longer be considered a national province
  • Sohar will no longer be considered a national province

B. Fight On

  • Stability -6
  • Lose 10000 troops in a random province
  • A random province revolts
  • Break vassalization with Oman
