280001. Chimgi-Tura or Qashliq

Sibir — Not random

Conditions

  • Own Chimgi Tura
  • Own Sibir
  • Control Sibir

Will happen within 5 days of January 2, 1500
Checked again every 5 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1510)

Description

When Tayguba founded the Khanate of Sibir, he created his capital in the vicinity of modern-day Tyumen. This was good as it was along the banks of the Tobol River, but was a poor defensive location. Murtaza and his brothers, Kuchum and Ahmed-Giray, moved the capital to Qashliq (Sibir), during their reigns, as it was a much safer location and was closer to the meeting place of the Tobol and Irtysh rivers. Of course, such a location also served to isolate khanate which was a blessing and a hinderance.

Actions

A. Qashliq it shall be!

  • Move capital to Sibir
  • +5000 cavalry in the capital province
  • -25 relations with Golden Horde
  • -10 relations with Uzbeks
  • -50 relations with Muscovy
  • -10 relations with a random country
  • -10 relations with a random country
  • Stability +2

B. Chimgi-Tura suits us fine!

  • Global revolt risk +2 for 12 months
  • Stability -1
  • +50

280000. The Formation of the Sibir Khanate

Sibir — Not random

Triggered by

Action A of 325008 - The Formation of the Sibir Khanate for Uzbeks

Description

Following the disintegration of the Horde, descendents of One Khan formed the Khanate of Sibir, near the modern day city of Tyumen. They quickly established their domain which existed from Kurgan in the West, to Ichim in the East. This khanate would survive well into the 16th century until it was extinguished by cossacks from Russia.

Actions

A. Go Sibir!

  • Change technology group to Muslim
  • tatar will become an accepted culture
  • altai will no longer be an accepted culture
  • Culture in Sibir changes to tatar
  • Stability +2
  • Move capital to Sibir
  • Chimgi Tura will be considered a national province
  • Ishim will be considered a national province
  • Irtis will be considered a national province
  • Ishim will be considered a national province
  • Akmola will be considered a national province
  • Ekibastu will be considered a national province
  • Kulunda will be considered a national province
  • -100 relations with Uzbeks
  • -100 relations with Golden Horde
