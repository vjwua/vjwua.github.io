167000. Kingdom of Finland is Born

Finland — Not random

Conditions

  • Own Nyland
  • Control Nyland
  • Country has at least 2 non-colonial provinces

Will happen within 15 days of January 2, 1720
Checked again every 15 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after December 31, 1819)

Description

While few people in the 18th and early 19th centuries entertained the idea of an independent Finland, under the right circumstances a Kingdom of Finland might have been established.

Actions

A. We are Finland!

  • Move capital to Nyland
  • nordic will become an accepted culture
  • Land tech investment: +1400
  • Naval tech investment: +700
  • Trade tech investment: +2000
  • Infrastructure tech investment: +1800
  • +150
  • Gain bailiff in the capital province
  • Gain bailiff in a random province
  • +10% inflation
  • +15000 infantry in the capital province
  • +7000 cavalry in the capital province
  • +10 artillery in the capital province

167004. Finnish Rule Over Karelians

Finland — Not random

Conditions

  • Own Arkhangyelsk
  • Control Arkhangyelsk
  • Event 167003 - Finnish Rule Over Karelians for Finland has already occurred

Will happen within 10000 days of June 7, 1720
Checked again every 10000 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after May 28, 1803)

Description

The Finnish and Karelian people are very close ethnically and especially linguistically. Some writers and ethnologists think that the Karelian people of the East have preserved the 'crucial Finnish element' which has been lost among the Finns after centuries of Western rule.

Actions

A. Great!

  • Culture in Arkhangyelsk changes to ugric
  • +200 population in Arkhangyelsk
  • Akerhus will be considered a claim province
