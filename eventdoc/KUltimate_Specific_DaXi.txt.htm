315000. Da Xi is independent

Da Xi — Not random

Conditions

  • None of the following must occur:
    • Event 147124 - Resistant leader declares an independent state for China has already occurred
    • Event 317001 - Resistant leader declares an independent state for Southern Ming has already occurred
  • At least one of the following must occur:
    • China exists
    • Southern Ming exists

Will happen within 10 days of February 2, 1633
Checked again every 10 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after July 2, 1644)

Description

Zhang Xian Zhong declares his independent state of Da Xi and declares himself the true emperor of China claiming the Ming dynasty no longer has its divine mandate having abused their power.

Actions

A. OK

  • Break vassalization with China
  • Break vassalization with Southern Ming
  • Event 147124 - Resistant leader declares an independent state for China will never fire
  • Event 317001 - Resistant leader declares an independent state for Southern Ming will never fire
  • Event 315001 - We openly declare war for Da Xi is triggered immediately
