310002. Raids on the Dendi

Mossi — Not random

Conditions

  • Songhai exists
  • Songhai owns Huanco
  • The following must not occur:
    • Mali is a vassal of Mossi

Will happen within 3000 days of January 2, 1419
Checked again every 3000 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1464)

Description

With the Mali on the retreat the Mossi were free to launch raids on their neighbors. They often raided the fertile lands of the Songhai.

Actions

A. To the victor go the spoils!

  • +25
  • Event 284005 - Mossi Raids for Songhai is triggered immediately
  • Break royal marriage with Songhai
  • Break vassalization with Songhai

310003. Return to the Old Ways

Mossi — Not random

Conditions

  • The following must not occur:
    • Centralization is at 5 or higher
  • Country has at least 3 non-colonial provinces
  • Country is controlled by AI

Will happen within 1000 days of January 2, 1425
Checked again every 1000 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1500)

Description

The Mossi were a primitive people compared to their neighbors. Although they were fierce warriors they were only fair administrators. When their empire grew too large to control directly they did as West Africans had done for centuries - let conquered people rule themselves so long as they sent tribute.

Actions

A. Demand tribute from our foes

  • +50
  • Grant independence to Bantus
  • Grant independence to Songhai
  • Grant independence to Ashanti
  • Grant independence to Hausa
  • Grant independence to Dahomey

B. This is the beginning of a new empire!

  • Centralization +1
  • Innovativeness +1
  • Stability -3
  • A random province revolts

310000. Mossi Expansionism

Mossi — Not random

Conditions

  • Event 218002 - The Mossi and the Songhai Empire for Mali has already occurred

Will happen within 30 days of January 2, 1460
Checked again every 30 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1492)

Description

As Songhai hesitated to destroy Mali the Mossi took advantage of the situation to expand their borders. They looked towards the lands of the Malinke.

Actions

A. Let us expand!

  • Bambuk will be considered a national province
  • +5000 infantry in the capital province
  • -150 relations with Mali

B. We are content as we are!

  • +75
  • Stability +1

310001. Ouagadougou's Revival

Mossi — Not random

Conditions

  • Event 284016 - The Mossi are Dispersed for Songhai has already occurred

Will happen within 360 days of January 2, 1550
Checked again every 360 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1650)

Description

The Mossi were never wholly defeated by the Songhai and when Songhai showed signs of weakness they took the opportunity to revive their state. The Kingdom of Ouagadougou broke away from the empire during the inept reign of Ishaq II, hastening the downfall of the Songhai.

Actions

A. We are free!

  • Culture in Kong changes to ashanti
  • Religion in Kong changes to pagan
  • Change religion to pagan
  • Stability +3
  • Break vassalization with Songhai
  • +2000 population in Kong
  • +1 base manpower in Kong
