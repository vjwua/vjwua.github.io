244001. The meeting in Kalundborg

Norway — Not random

Conditions

  • At least one of the following must occur:
    • Event 159012 - The fate of Norway for Denmark has already occurred
    • Event 159013 - The fate of Norway for Denmark has already occurred
  • Norway is a vassal of Denmark
  • Country is not at war

Will happen within 360 days of January 2, 1494
Checked again every 360 days until trigger is met (cannot happen after January 2, 1513)
unless prevented by
Action B of 159012 - The fate of Norway for Denmark
Action B of 159013 - The fate of Norway for Denmark

Description

While Hans ruled Denmark and Norway, his mother Dorothea tried to convince him that he should share his Kingdoms with his younger brother, Frederik. She suggested that Hans should give Norway to Duke Frederik of Holstein. Hans had no desire to part with Norway however, so in 1494 he called for a Assembly of the Estates of the Realm in Kalundborg. Here it was declared that the Kingdoms of Denmark and Norway should forever be one.

Actions

A. Accept the declaration (End Game)

  • Event 159014 - Norway accepts the declaration at Kalundborg for Denmark is triggered immediately

B. Reject the declaration

  • Stability -7
  • -100 relations with Denmark
  • +50 relations with Holstein
  • +25 relations with Sweden
  • Innovativeness +1
  • Centralization +1
  • Event 159015 - Norway rejects the declaration at Kalundborg for Denmark is triggered immediately
